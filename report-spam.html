What Is The Offer Email Suppression List?

As a responsible online marketing company with several thousand affiliated publishers promoting our products daily we voluntarily operate a global suppression database (similar to a Do-Not-Call registry for phones) which we require all marketers with our web-offers to remove from their newsletters and email campaigns. Add your email address to the suppression list by entering your address into the form and pressing submit.

Reporting Unsolicited Spam / Email Abuse

We take the misuse of email marketing by our affiliates very seriously. If you feel you have received an email from one of our affiliate publishers that violates any type of local or federal law or they have not honored your request to be removed promptly please come forward.

Assist us in resolving the issue by providing the full email, including headers, so that we may terminate their account and withhold payment.

Please forward all email inquiries or abuse reports to support@creditrelief.us.com and include as much information as possible in your email body.

Contacting Us by Postal Mail

As part of our commitment to responsible email marketing we require all affiliated publishers to include not only their own contact information but also our information.
