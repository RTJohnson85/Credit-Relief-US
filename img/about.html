About Us

The American debt crisis has reached immense proportions. Today, the average American carries almost $100,000 in debt between auto loans, mortgages, credit cards, and student debt. It’s a terrible place to find yourself, and it can feel impossible to get out of it.

At U.S. Credit Relief, we specialize in working with people just like you – struggling to make ends meet, crushed beneath debt, dealing with creditor harassment, and more. We can create a customized action plan that speaks directly to your financial situation and helps you find the relief that you need.

We can help you:
  • Stop harassing creditor communications
  • Reduce the amount you owe
  • Consolidate and pay just one monthly payment
  • Repay priority creditors immediately
  • Reduce the interest rate and unlock equity to pay down debts



Our Mission

Our mission is simple – to help you find financial relief, whatever that means in your specific situation. We’ve worked with consumers struggling to pay high credit card debts, those on the verge of bankruptcy, and people dealing with dozens of creditors to find peace of mind and financial relief.

We can help you stop living paycheck to paycheck and put an end to the stress of dealing with creditors and debt collectors.

Every strategy is customized based on your unique situation, needs, and goals. We invite you to contact us today to speak with one of our expert consultants. We are here to help you solve your debt challenges and retake control over your financial life.
